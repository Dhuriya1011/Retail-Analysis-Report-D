# Retail Sales Analysis Report

## Introduction

Retail businesses generate large amounts of sales data that can be analyzed to improve decision-making. This project focuses on analyzing monthly sales across multiple product categories.

## Objective

To apply data science techniques to a real-world retail dataset and identify patterns, trends, and key performance indicators.

## Methodology

1. Data Collection
2. Data Cleaning
3. Statistical Analysis
4. Visualization
5. Insight Generation

## Results

### Sales Trend

Sales showed continuous growth from January to October.

### Category Performance

Electronics emerged as the highest-performing category, contributing the largest share of revenue.

### Business Insights

* Increased sales indicate growing customer demand.
* Electronics products are the primary revenue driver.
* Clothing sales demonstrate steady growth potential.
* Grocery sales provide a stable revenue base.

## Conclusion

This project successfully applied data science techniques to a real-world retail dataset. The findings demonstrate how businesses can use data analytics to monitor performance and make informed decisions.

## Learning Outcomes

* Real-world data analysis
* Data visualization
* Trend identification
* Business insight generation
* Practical application of data science concepts
